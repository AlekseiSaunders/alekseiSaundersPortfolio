Problem solver, creator, and birdwatcher.

In my previous career as a zookeeper I found that many conservation, welfare, and husbandry programs had trouble connecting to a larger audience.

I learned to code in an attempt to bring awareness to species that weren't the stars of the show. Starting with Adobe Dreamweaver© in 2006, I built a site highlighting the fish of Madagascar, and implemented the Google Maps API to add custom maps to the site.

Since then I've built and maintained Wordpress and Squarespace sites to highlight my photography business as well as my love of beer and brewing. While those sites are no longer active, I quickly found that WYSIWYG editors were less than satisfying; a fact that inspired my desire to become a software engineer.

About Me

Currently

In 2020 I began to investigate a more structured learning path to become a Software Engineer. The resources I found were astounding, and I began my community-supported learning in 2021 with Codecademy and Scrimba. I've been lucky enough to find myself in some very supportive and nurturing communities along the way.

In early 2022 I ended my career as a zookeeper to pursue a future as a Software Engineer and Full-Stack Web Developer.
