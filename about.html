I've been coding since 2006

In my previous career as a zookeeper I found that many conservation, welfare, and husbandry programs had trouble connecting to a larger audience.

I learned to code in an attempt to bring awareness to species that weren't the stars of the show. Starting with Adobe Dreamweaver© in 2006, I built a site highlighting the fish of Madagascar, and implemented the Google Maps API to add custom maps to the site.

About Me

Problem solver, creator, and birdwatcher.

Since then I've built and maintained Wordpress and Squarespace sites to highlight my photography business as well as my love of beer and brewing. While those sites are no longer active, I quickly found that WYSIWYG editors were less than satisfying.

In 2020 I began to investigate a more structured learning path for Software Engineering. The resources found were astounding and I began my community-supported learning in 2021 with Codecademy and Scrimba. I've been lucky enough to find myself in some very supportive and nurturing communities along the way.

In early 2022 I ended my career as a zookeeper to pursue Software Engineering and Web Development full time.
